## Always bring

- Mask
- Fins
- Regulator
- BCD
- Computer
- Lamp
- Camera (extra battery)
- Logbook
- Drinking water
- Towel
- Aquamed card

## Bring when necessary

- Flag
- C-cards
- SMB
- Slate / wetnotes

## Cold water

- Drysuit, hood, gloves
- 12kg weights

## Warm water

- Wetsuit
- 5kg weights
- Neoprene boots
